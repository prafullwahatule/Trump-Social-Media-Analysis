## ğŸ” Key Insights

* **Twitter dominates engagement**, generating ~**2.3B interactions**, compared to **Truth Social (~0.5B)**.
* **Original posts contribute ~82.5%** of total content, driving higher engagement.
* **Only ~3.1% posts are deleted**, indicating stable posting behavior.
* **2020 recorded the highest engagement**, reflecting peak audience interest.
* **October shows maximum monthly engagement**, followed by November & September.
* **Media-based posts deliver significantly higher engagement** than text-only posts.
